Slide size: 1280x720.

Visual system:
- White background.
- Cyan-blue top header band and footer band borrowed from the reference deck.
- Orange-red vertical accent beside titles.
- Page number centered in the footer.

Typography:
- Chinese/UI sans: PingFang SC, Microsoft YaHei, Arial fallback.
- Cover title 42-52px, body slide title 24-30px, body text 17-24px, footer 11px.

Palette:
- Main blue: #08A8DC
- Light blue: #78D5EF
- Deep blue: #1D5E8C
- Accent orange: #F05A28
- Body text: #222831
- Muted text: #607080
- Light panel: #F4F8FB

Chart/table grammar:
- Use direct labels where possible.
- Use blue for LEO/MEO baseline evidence and orange for GEO or warning/long-tail emphasis.
- Tables use dark-blue header, light row separators, no heavy grid.

Footer/source grammar:
- Bottom bar with centered page number.
- Short source note left above footer only when needed.

Allowed layout families:
- cover with metric rail
- title + evidence table
- left method text + right diagram
- full-width figure with callout rail
- split figure + compact table
- conclusion grid

Banned motifs:
- decorative icons without data meaning
- invented official logos or satellite brand marks
- dense paragraphs that duplicate the report
